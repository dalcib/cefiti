# Product Guidelines

## Prose and Messaging
- **Tone:** Formal and Regulatory.
- **Style:** Precise language following legal and governmental standards. Prioritize official terminology to ensure compliance and authoritative accuracy.

## Visual Identity
- **Brand Standards:** Strict adherence to the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock (MAPA) branding, including official logos, designated color palettes, and standard typography.
- **Consistency:** Ensure all UI elements align with the visual identity used in other official agricultural tools.

## Usability and Accessibility
- **Offline First:** Provide clear visual feedback regarding data synchronization. The user must always know if they are working with cached data and when the last update occurred.
- **Connectivity Awareness:** Ensure the interface remains functional and responsive even in areas with poor or no internet connectivity.

## Communication and Updates
- **Transparency:** Prominent display of the current database version and its effective date.
- **Traceability:** Users should be able to easily verify that they are using the most current set of regulations.

## Regulatory Context
- **Clarity:** Provide simplified, plain-language summaries alongside formal legal text to help users interpret complex regulatory exceptions quickly without losing the necessary legal rigor.

# Initial Concept
Catálogo de Exigências Fitossanitárias para o Trânsito Interestadual (CEFiTI).

# Product Guide

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Agricultural Inspectors and Fiscal Auditors who need to verify compliance during interstate transport inspections.

## Product Goals
- **Core Goal:** Provide quick, reliable access to phytosanitary requirements for interstate transport of agricultural goods.
- **Success Criteria:** High accuracy in matching specific transport scenarios (host, origin, destination) with the correct regulatory requirements.

## Key Features
- **Search Engine:** Query requirements based on Host (Hospedeiro), Origin State (Origem), and Destination State (Destino).

## Design & Experience
- **Visual Style:** Adherence to the official style guidelines of the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock (MAPA).
- **UX Priority:** Optimized for professional use, ensuring clarity and precision in presenting regulatory data.
